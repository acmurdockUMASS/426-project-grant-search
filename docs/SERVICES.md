## grant-search-service: 
* Allows users to search for grants with text based queries. <br>
## user-profile-service:
* Allows users to manage their own information and have quick access to tracked grants. <br>
## grant-tracking-service:
* Tracks grant milestones such as application opening, submission deadline ect and notifies users when that milestone is upcoming.
## grant elligiblity-analysis-service
* Evaluates whether an organization appears eligibale for a grant based on the organization's information and the grant's requirements.
