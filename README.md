## Domain:

  The system is meant to assist nonprofit companies, and grant dependent services easier accessibility and matching to federal loans that match their mission. The system simulates the challenge of serving multiple users at once, processing eligibility matching requests, delivering personalized grant recommendations, and notifying organizations when funding opportunities change or deadlines approach. A single server can become a bottleneck as thousands of organizations search, filter, and request eligibility analyses during periods of high demand. This makes caching async processing, load balancing, and resilient service design essential.
